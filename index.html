PulseRoots: Electronic Music Styles Tree

How to Explore

Welcome to PulseRoots! This guide helps you navigate the vast world of electronic music using our interactive tools.

On desktop, you'll find an immersive treemap that visually maps out the connections between genres. On mobile, the genres are organized into a clear and accessible accordion list.

Styles and Substyles

Both views start with broad "Families" (like Techno or House) and branch down into specific styles and substyles. Click or tap on any genre to open a panel with a detailed description and a sample track to help you understand its unique sound.

Note on Classification

This visualization represents one interpretation of how electronic music genres are categorized. Music is fluid, and other classifications might group styles differently based on cultural, historical, or sonic perspectives.

Inspirations

PulseRoots is inspired by the incredible work of other music exploration projects. Special thanks to Ishkur's Guide to Electronic Music, Musicmap, and Every Noise at Once for paving the way.

Listening to Tracks

To listen to the example tracks, a Spotify account may be required. The embedded player provides a 30-second preview for all users. Logging into Spotify may unlock the full track, depending on your subscription.
