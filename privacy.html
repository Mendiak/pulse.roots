Last Updated: 16 de enero de 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PulseRoots ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information that we obtain from visitors to our website, https://mendiak.github.io/pulse.roots/ (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information from our users through the Site. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes or store any personal data on our servers.

Our contact form is powered by Web3Forms. When you submit the contact form, the information you provide (name, email, message) is sent directly to Web3Forms and then forwarded to us via email. We do not store this information on our Site's server. Please refer to Web3Forms' privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how our Site is being used. Google Analytics collects anonymous data about your visit, such as your IP address (anonymized), browser type, operating system, referral source, pages visited, and time spent on the Site. This information is used to improve the functionality and content of the Site. Google's ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your visits to the Site is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Links to Other Websites

Our Site contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked sites you visit.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
